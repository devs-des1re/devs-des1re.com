## **Full Color Palette**

### **1. Base**

* **Background:** `#F5F5F5`
* **Primary Text:** `#000000`
* **Secondary Text:** `#333333`
* **Muted Text:** `#666666`

---

### **2. Surfaces**

* **Surface Primary (cards, panels, modals):** `#FFFFFF`
* **Surface Secondary (nested panels):** `#FAFAFA`
* **Surface Hover:** `#F0F0F0`

---

### **3. Accent System**

* **Primary Accent:** `#0099FF`
* **Accent Hover / Active:** `#007ACC`
* **Success:** `#28A745`
* **Warning:** `#FFC107`
* **Error:** `#FF6B6B`

---

### **4. Borders**

* **Light Border:** `#E0E0E0`
* **Dark Border:** `#CCCCCC`

---

### **5. Shadows**

* **Shadow Light:** `rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.05)`
* **Shadow Medium:** `rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.1)`
